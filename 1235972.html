Within 30 days

A full refund or exchange can be offered on all items returned in a re-saleable condition (unopened and pristine) within 30 days of purchase, the customer is asked to retain their proof of purchase. The original receipt must be scanned as part of the refund/exchange process. ? Customers returning items without proof of purchase maybe offered an exchange to the current retail price of the item, a store manager may use their discretion regarding a refund.

Within 1 year

If a customer returns items outside the 30 days then an exchange may be offered, the customer is asked to retain their proof of purchase. The original receipt must be scanned as part of the refund/exchange process. ? A repair maybe offered, if applicable, most large purchases (e.g. Pressure Washers, Lawnmowers etc) come complete with a warranty from the Supplier, a service call should be booked by the customer as specified in the manual. ? Own Brand items can be reviewed at store level, please refer to Category updates on how best to support our Customers (repair or replacement within 1 year of purchase).
